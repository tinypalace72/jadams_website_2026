John Adams

Vancouver-based picture editor

John is an award-nominated picture editor with over two decades of experience cutting documentary series, feature docs, narrative drama, and branded content for the world's leading broadcasters and streaming platforms.

His work spans a wide range of subjects — from wildlife and nature to true crime, historical docs, and music biography — with a consistent focus on character-driven storytelling and precise, purposeful rhythm in the edit.

Based in Vancouver, BC. Available for projects worldwide.
